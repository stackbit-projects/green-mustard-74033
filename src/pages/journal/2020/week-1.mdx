# dot13 - Mineral Jam

Dot13 is a young Romanian artist currently living in Vienna, Austria. He’s trying to pursue his dream of becoming a music producer. His sound is set in a modern post-minimal scenario with a funky-groove twist. This track will take you on a 11 minute trip!
